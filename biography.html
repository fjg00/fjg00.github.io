about me

Short Biography

I grew up in Lebanon and completed my undergraduate studies at the American University of Beirut (AUB), where I earned a B.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a 4.0 CGPA and Dean's Honor List distinction. During my time at AUB, I spent a summer at EPFL as an E3 research intern at the Adaptive Systems Laboratory, where I worked with Ali H. Sayed and Mert Kayaalp on multi-agent reinforcement learning in decentralized POMDPs. I am currently pursuing a Direct PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto.
