A bit of context

I am a Direct-PhD student in the Systems Control Group at the University of Toronto, working with Mireille E. Broucke.

My current work focuses on system identification for stable linear time invariant systems, mainly through adaptive observer constructions and tools from systems and control.

Before Toronto, I completed a B.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the American University of Beirut (CGPA 4.0, Dean’s Honor List), and spent a summer at the Adaptive Systems Laboratory at EPFL as an E3 research intern working on multi-agent reinforcement learning in decentralized POMDPs.
