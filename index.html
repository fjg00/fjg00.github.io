what I’m doing now

Right now

  • Working on an indirect identification scheme for stable LTI systems based on adaptive observers.
  • Polishing a submission on this topic for IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control.
  • TA duties in control / robotics courses at UofT.
  • Thinking about how structure in observers can give cleaner identification guarantees.
research

Things I care about

  • Adaptive observer–based identification for stable linear systems, with explicit convergence and robustness guarantees.
  • Decentralized learning under partial observability (policy evaluation, belief sharing, communication constraints).
  • Shared control and HMI for automated driving: handover, driver intention, stop–and–go scenarios.
  • Data from teaching in large intro programming courses and what it says about how people actually learn.
