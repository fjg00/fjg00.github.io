About Me

I am a Direct-PhD student in the Systems Control Group at the University of Toronto, advised by Professor Mireille E. Broucke.

My research focuses on system identification for stable linear time-invariant systems, using adaptive observers and classical systems and control theory.

Prior to UofT, I completed a B.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the American University of Beirut (CGPA 4.0, Dean's Honor List), and spent a summer at the Adaptive Systems Laboratory at EPFL as an E3 research intern working on multi-agent reinforcement learning in decentralized POMDPs.
