about

Short bio

I am a Direct-PhD student in the Systems Control Group at the University of Toronto. My current work focuses on indirect identification of stable linear time invariant systems via adaptive observers.

Before Toronto, I completed a B.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the American University of Beirut, graduating with a 4.0 CGPA and Dean’s Honor List recognition. I spent a summer as an E3 research intern at the Adaptive Systems Laboratory, EPFL, working on multi-agent reinforcement learning in decentralized POMDPs.
