About Me

I am a Direct-PhD Candidate in the Systems Control Group at the University of Toronto, advised by Professor Mireille E. Broucke.

My research focuses on developing a framework for continuous-time system identification for stable linear time-invariant systems using concepts from adaptive control, optimization, and advanced linear algebra
