Research & Publications

Indirect Identification of Stable Linear Systems using an Adaptive Observer 2025
Submitted to IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control (TAC)

Proposes an indirect identification scheme for stable LTI systems based on an adaptive observer construction, with convergence and robustness guarantees.

Keywords: Stable LTI systems, adaptive observers, system identification
Policy Evaluation in Decentralized POMDPs with Belief Sharing 2023
IEEE Open Journal for Control Systems

Studies policy evaluation in decentralized POMDPs where agents share beliefs rather than full observations, and analyzes how this impacts performance.

Keywords: Decentralized POMDPs, multi-agent RL, belief sharing
