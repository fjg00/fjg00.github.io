Where this comes from

I am part of the Systems Control Group at the University of Toronto, working with Mireille E. Broucke. My current work combines ideas from classical systems and control, observer design, and system identification.

Before that, I worked at the Adaptive Systems Laboratory (EPFL) on multi-agent reinforcement learning in decentralized POMDPs, and completed my B.E. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the American University of Beirut.
