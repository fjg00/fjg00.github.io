Policy Evaluation in Decentralized POMDPs with Belief Sharing
M. Kayaalp, F. J. Ghadieh, A. H. Sayed
IEEE Open Journal of Control Systems, vol. 2, pp. 125–145, 2023

We consider cooperative policy evaluation where agents cannot observe the environment state directly. We propose a fully decentralized belief forming strategy that relies on individual updates and localized interactions over a communication network. Agents exchange both beliefs and value function parameter estimates, allowing information to diffuse over the network. We analytically show that this yields agent parameters with a bounded difference from a centralized baseline.
